Privacy Policy

Last modified: 07/28/16

Swappl is a simple and easy way to share contact information and social media handles. Due to the nature of the app, we understand that this will affect your privacy, so we want to be as clear as possible regarding the way we handle your information.

Swappl asks for a variety of personal information, including your name, photo, phone number and email address, as well as social media handles, such as Facebook, Snapchat, Instagram and Twitter. Swappl values your privacy, and as such, all the information you enter is stored locally on your device. Swappl does not upload your information to a server, and Swappl as a company does not have direct access to your information. Furthermore, Swappl utilizes Bluetooth and/or local Wifi connection to share your information with the people you meet, so that your information does not go through our servers. Swappl will never sell your information and will not share it except with the people you choose.

Swappl does its absolute best to protect your information and ensure that it cannot be obtained unless shared by you. However, Swappl is not responsible for stolen information in any form, and you accept the dangers inherent in all technological communication when using our services. By using Swappl, you agree to waive and release Swappl and all Swappl employees from any liability for claims resulting from your use of the app.
